        <h2>Server Rules</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>Respect Others:</strong> Treat everyone with respect. No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.</li>
            <li><strong>No Cheating:</strong> Any form of cheating or exploiting is strictly prohibited.</li>
            <li><strong>Roleplay:</strong> Stay in character at all times. Breaking roleplay is not allowed.</li>
            <li><strong>No Random Killing (RDM):</strong> Do not kill other players without valid roleplay reasons.</li>
            <li><strong>No Vehicle Deathmatching (VDM):</strong> Do not use vehicles to randomly kill players.</li>
            <li><strong>Follow Staff Instructions:</strong> Listen to server admins and moderators. Their decisions are final.</li>
            <li><strong>No Advertising:</strong> Do not advertise other servers or services.</li>
            <li><strong>English Only:</strong> Use English in public chats.</li>
            <li><strong>No Offensive Content:</strong> Do not share offensive, explicit, or inappropriate content.</li>
            <li><strong>Report Issues:</strong> Report any bugs, exploits, or rule violations to staff immediately.</li>
            <li><strong>Community Events:</strong> Participate in server events responsibly and follow specific event rules.</li>
            <li><strong>No Spamming:</strong> Avoid spamming chat with unnecessary messages, links, or commands.</li>
            <li><strong>Keep Roleplay Realistic:</strong> Avoid unrealistic scenarios or actions that break immersion.</li>
            <li><strong>Mic Use:</strong> Ensure clear and non-disruptive communication if using a microphone.</li>
            <li><strong>Property Respect:</strong> Do not damage or misuse player-owned properties without consent.</li>
            <li><strong>Combat Logging:</strong> Do not disconnect from the server during active roleplay or combat situations to avoid consequences.</li>
            <li><strong>Character Development:</strong> Ensure your character has a realistic backstory and behaves consistently within the roleplay setting.</li>
            <li><strong>Vehicle Rules:</strong> Drive responsibly and adhere to traffic laws unless roleplay dictates otherwise.</li>
            <li><strong>Emergency Services Respect:</strong> Respect the roleplay of emergency services such as police, EMS, and firefighters.</li>
            <li><strong>No Meta-Gaming:</strong> Do not use out-of-character information to influence in-character decisions.</li>
            <li><strong>No Power-Gaming:</strong> Avoid forcing actions on others or creating scenarios that give your character unrealistic advantages.</li>
            <li><strong>Gang and Faction Rules:</strong> Gang or faction activity must be approved and adhere to roleplay standards. Unauthorized groups will face consequences.</li>
            <li><strong>Weapon Usage:</strong> Use weapons responsibly within roleplay scenarios. Avoid random shootings or brandishing weapons without purpose.</li>
            <li><strong>No OOC Trolling:</strong> Do not disrupt gameplay with out-of-character (OOC) comments or behavior meant to annoy or provoke others.</li>
            <li><strong>Respect Server Economy:</strong> Do not exploit the in-game economy or engage in actions that harm its balance, such as unrealistic trades or item duplication.</li>
            <li><strong>Use Common Sense:</strong> If something feels wrong or disruptive, it likely is. Consider the impact of your actions on the server community.</li>
            <li><strong>Respect Private RP:</strong> Avoid interrupting or interfering with private roleplay scenarios unless invited or directly involved.</li>
            <li><strong>Server Boundaries:</strong> Do not exploit map boundaries or glitches to access restricted areas or gain an unfair advantage.</li>
            <li><strong>New Player Protection:</strong> Treat new players respectfully and assist them in learning server rules and systems.</li>
            <li><strong>Stream Sniping:</strong> Do not use live streams or external sources to gain an advantage over other players.</li>
            <li><strong>Roleplay Injuries:</strong> Roleplay the consequences of injuries realistically. Avoid actions like returning to conflict zones immediately after being revived.</li>
            <li><strong>No Unrealistic Vehicles:</strong> Avoid using vehicles that break immersion, such as flying cars or excessively modified vehicles without approval.</li>
            <li><strong>Time Sync:</strong> Ensure actions and events align with the in-game timeline and respect continuity.</li>
            <li><strong>Server Conflict Resolution:</strong> Resolve disputes through in-game mechanisms or with staff assistance. Avoid public arguments.</li>
            <li><strong>Illegal Activities:</strong> While roleplay may include illegal acts, ensure they are within realistic boundaries and approved server guidelines.</li>
            <li><strong>Non-Player Characters (NPCs):</strong> Interact with NPCs realistically. Exploiting NPC mechanics for personal gain is prohibited.</li>
            <li><strong>Business Operations:</strong> Player-owned businesses must operate within server rules and respect economic balance.</li>
            <li><strong>Heist and Robbery Rules:</strong> Follow specific guidelines for heists and robberies, including required player participation and cooldown periods.</li>
            <li><strong>Housing Rules:</strong> Respect property ownership. Breaking into homes requires valid roleplay and staff approval.</li>
            <li><strong>Drug and Weapon Trafficking:</strong> Engage in illegal trades responsibly and within server-approved systems.</li>
            <li><strong>Emergency Vehicle Use:</strong> Only authorized personnel may use emergency vehicles. Unauthorized use will result in penalties.</li>
            <li><strong>Event Participation:</strong> Follow event-specific rules. Disrupting events will lead to removal and potential bans.</li>
            <li><strong>Gang Wars:</strong> All gang conflicts must be approved by staff and follow predefined rules to ensure fairness.</li>
            <li><strong>Political Roleplay:</strong> Political campaigns or rallies must respect server decorum and avoid inflammatory content.</li>
            <li><strong>Server Lore:</strong> Adhere to established server lore. Major changes or disruptions must be coordinated with staff.</li>
        </ul>
        <h2>Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)</h2>
        <ul>
            <li><strong>What should I do if I encounter a rule-breaker?</strong> Report the player to a staff member with evidence if possible.</li>
            <li><strong>Can I apply for a staff position?</strong> Yes, check our Discord or website for application details.</li>
            <li><strong>Where can I find updates or announcements?</strong> All server updates and announcements are shared on our Discord server.</li>
            <li><strong>What happens if I break a rule?</strong> Consequences vary from warnings to permanent bans depending on the severity.</li>
            <li><strong>How can I suggest new features or changes?</strong> Join our Discord and post your suggestions in the appropriate channel.</li>
            <li><strong>Are mods or scripts allowed?</strong> Only approved mods and scripts are allowed. Check the server guidelines for details.</li>
            <li><strong>Can I join the server with friends?</strong> Yes, we encourage group roleplay as long as all members follow server rules.</li>
            <li><strong>What are the server's peak activity hours?</strong> Peak hours are typically in the evening (GMT). Check Discord for real-time activity updates.</li>
            <li><strong>How do I appeal a ban?</strong> You can appeal by filling out the ban appeal form on our website or contacting staff via Discord.</li>
            <li><strong>What should I do if I encounter a bug?</strong> Report bugs in the appropriate Discord channel with a detailed description and screenshots, if possible.</li>
            <li><strong>Are there any age restrictions for joining?</strong> Yes, the minimum age is 16. Exceptions can be made for mature roleplayers upon staff review.</li>
            <li><strong>Can I create custom content for the server?</strong> Yes, contact staff with your proposal to get approval for custom content such as skins, vehicles, or scripts.</li>
            <li><strong>What is the server's policy on roleplay realism?</strong> All roleplay must strive for realism and immersion. Unrealistic actions may result in warnings or penalties.</li>
            <li><strong>Do I need a microphone to join?</strong> A microphone is recommended for immersive roleplay but not mandatory. Ensure you can communicate effectively if needed.</li>
        </ul>
